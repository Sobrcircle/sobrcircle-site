Account

Delete your SobrCircle account

This page explains how to request account deletion and what happens after your request is verified.

How to request deletion

For account security, deletion requests must be verified. The current supported method is the signed-in in-app flow:

  1. Open SobrCircle.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Complete the required confirmation and re-authentication steps.

What is deleted

  • Account profile data
  • User-generated content such as check-ins, comments, messages, and reactions
  • Uploaded media files tied to the account, including profile photos, post and journal media under `users/{uid}/...`, and media stored for deleted chat threads
  • Notification tokens and app account records
  • Associated app metadata needed only for active account operation

What may be retained

Some limited records may be retained where required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Accounting or tax requirements
  • Dispute resolution or legal claims

Current retention windows include:

  • Backup copies: up to 35 days
  • Security and abuse logs: up to 12 months
  • Billing, tax, and transaction records: up to 7 years where required
  • Deletion-request audit records: up to 24 months
  • Legal hold or dispute records: until resolved

Deletion timeline

  • Account access is disabled immediately after successful verification.
  • Deletion from active systems is completed within 30 days.

Subscription notice

Deleting your SobrCircle account does not automatically cancel App Store or Google Play subscriptions. Subscriptions must be canceled in your Apple or Google account settings.

Support contact

If you need help with the deletion flow, contact support@sobrcircle.com.
